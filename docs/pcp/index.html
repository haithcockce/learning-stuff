Terminology and Concepts

  • Metrics are organized hierarchically into tree-like structures. Levels in the trees refine the relationships between metrics within those subtrees. For example, paging activity would be a child of memory in general while page scanning and major faults would be children of pageing activity.

  • Domain A logical grouping of metrics typically based on source such as NFS Client, Nginx, Windows, Linux (kernel), etc
